Executive Summary

ICLVS outlines a modular architecture that applies AI-assisted reasoning to analyze documents, detect governing jurisdiction, adapt clauses to the relevant normative framework, and preserve audit-grade traceability — while keeping human legal oversight central to the process.

  • Faster verification (conceptual projection)
  • Fewer drafting inconsistencies
  • Improved clause uniformity across jurisdictions

Use Cases

Regulatory document verification Land & real-estate registries Multi-jurisdictional contract adaptation Financial compliance (AML / privacy) Academic & institutional research (RegTech)

System Flow

Proposed procedural sequence for contextual legal verification.

  1. (100) Document Upload
  2. (200) Jurisdiction Detection
  3. (300) Normative / Clause Adaptation
  4. (400) Legal Traceability
  5. (500) Attorney Review → (600) Verified Output
