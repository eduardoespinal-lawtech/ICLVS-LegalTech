Purpose & Overview

This project proposes an intelligent framework to automate legal document verification and compliance across jurisdictions. It enhances transparency, accelerates review cycles, and promotes consistency through contextual reasoning and normative adaptation.

System Workflow

The process follows a structured path: Document Ingest → Contextual Reasoning → Normative Adaptation → Traceability & Audit, ensuring verifiable and explainable outputs.
