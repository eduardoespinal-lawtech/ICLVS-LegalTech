Developed by Eduardo Luis Espinal de Aza, Esq.

A Legal-Tech Demonstrative Framework for AI-Driven Regulatory Compliance.

Purpose & Overview

This project proposes an intelligent framework to automate legal document verification and compliance across jurisdictions. It enhances transparency, accelerates review cycles, and promotes consistency through contextual reasoning and normative adaptation.

Workflow

Upload → Contextual Reasoning → Normative Adaptation → Legal Traceability → Attorney Review (human-in-the-loop).

Steps

  1. Document ingestion & metadata detection.
  2. Contextual legal reasoning (jurisdiction, topic, risk).
  3. Normative adaptation (modeled references).
  4. Traceability record & audit hash.
  5. Attorney review & approval.

Aligned with transparency, explainability, and human oversight requirements.

Architecture

Modular components enabling multi-jurisdictional compliance and verifiable audit trails.

Modules

  • Context Core: contextual retrieval & reasoning.
  • Adaptation Engine: clause mapping to target jurisdiction.
  • Traceability System: audit log & hash generation.
  • Review Interface: attorney validation & overrides.
